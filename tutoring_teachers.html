Got Questions?

Are we taking commissions?

Yes, we are taking commissions. However, we would like to stress that our tutor pay is already on the higher end of the market range in Malaysia. Resources are also required to keep the website online and run this service efficiently. 

Which student am I going to be matched with? Who makes the choice? 

You have the freedom to choose your students! Whenever new students sign up, tutors will get to pick who they want to teach based on factors such as language requirement and subject choice.

What are my responsibilities?

All you have to do is to teach well and record your student's attendance. We will fully handle the payment of fees and other managerial duties. 

Can I take on more than one student?

Sure! In fact, most of our tutors take on many students simultaneously.
